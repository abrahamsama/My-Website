About Me

I am a class of '22 Computer Science major originally from Eagle Pass, Tx. Coming from a border town, I've come to appreciate all the people and resources that have become available to me since I started attending Texas A&M.

I'm involved in plenty extracurricular activities ranging from professional to casual, including Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE), LEGION Men's Org, and Percussion Studio. I handpicked these organizations freshman and sophomore year based on the networking opportunities and overall enjoyment I get out of them. While involved, I made some great friends and good people skills that have prepared me to collaborate in the work force when the time comes.

Even with all these extracurricular activities, I still found some time to code. I'm apart of the SHPE Tech Affairs Committee which has taught me HTML, CSS, and Google Sheet Script, and I have attended numerous hackathons in which I've used similar skills (more on this in portfolio).

I believe I work really well in a group environment and that is because I have this genuine love and respect for people. Understanding that humans thrive better with the help of other humans is the first step into entering a successful career, and I feel like this fundamental skill sets me apart from other Computer Science majors and engineers. Although I can learn and function on my own without the aid of others, I know that I can accomplish a whole lot more when I collaborate with other people.
